Safety Guidelines

  • Sprints: Always warm up thoroughly. Start at 70% intensity week 1
  • Jumps: Focus on landing mechanics before height/distance
  • Throws: Use appropriate weight (3-10lbs typically)
  • All movements: Stop if form breaks down or pain occurs
